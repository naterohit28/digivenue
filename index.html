💡 How It Works

Bank (Invoice) portion = GST base amounts + GST collected

Cash portion = Total deal − bank-invoiced base

Formula:
Base₁₈ = GST18 ÷ 0.18
Base₅ = GST5 ÷ 0.05

Cash = Total − Base₁₈ − Base₅
